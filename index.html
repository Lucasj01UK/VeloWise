Bikes

There is a vast array of bicycle brands around the globe, and it's hard to get away from the well-established powerhouses. Giant, Specialized and Cannondale are fantastic brands but, you see them everywhere. Riding solo or in a club, spotting another one is almost certain. Seldom you will feast your eyes upon an ORRO, Field or Enigma. These steeds are rare indeed, but can be found at similar prices and are just as reliable, beautiful and obtainable. Click here to see the biggest list of British Bicycle brands and frame builders.

Accessories

Cycling accessories. The essential kit you need to aid your commute, training or race. To make your weekend away in the wilderness more comfortable. The items you must have to separate your bike from the crowd. Accessorising your bike is great fun and can cost very little. Click here to see the biggest range of British cycling accessories.

Components

Components, critical parts that make the machine. Without them, we are going nowhere. Here is a list of almost all bicycle components that are owned by British companies. A favourite for us here at VeloWise is HOPE. They truly stand out as market leaders. Almost all their products are made on-site in Barnoldswick, Lancashire. Their products are renowned for being robust, affordable and reliable. A true testament to British engineering.

Clothing

While compiling this list of cycling apparel companies, I was astonished at how many there were, how obscure they are, and how hard it is to pin down where they are based and made. I have found over fifty already with more being added every month. Click here to see the biggest list of cycling clothing brands based here in the UK.

Insurance

Insuring your pride and joy is often overlooked. Don't get caught out and check out these Bike insurance packages , most of which, cover more than just the bike. Companies such as Yellow Jersey have 3 packages, the Essentials package aimed at leisure riding will cover Bike theft, accidental damage and vandalism. The Performance package is for the enthusiast rider who is part of a club, rides sportives, or takes their bike on trips abroad. Finally, there is the Ultimate package, the rider that competes in the UK or abroad needs cover for their equipment in the pit or transition area or needs public liability should consider looking into this package.

Nutrition

Peanutbutter and Banana on toast with a burning hot cup of tea is a VeloWise post-ride favourite, especially when it's cold. But what about the pre and mid-ride food your body requires for a quality training ride? From energy gels to food prep companies, here you will find a comprehensive list of places to find things to fill your cakehole, keep you energised and help you recover.
